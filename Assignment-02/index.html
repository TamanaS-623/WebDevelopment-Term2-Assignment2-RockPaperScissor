---------- Lets play together "Rock - Paper - Scissor" Game ----------

"Rock - Paper - Scissor" is a game like flipping a coin, rolling a dice is which each player (You and computer) has to select one of the three shapes. These shapes are rock, paper and scissor. In this game, paper wins over rock, rock wins over scissor and finally scissor wins over paper as shown in the image.
